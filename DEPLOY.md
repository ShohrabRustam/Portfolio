# 🚀 QUICK DEPLOY GUIDE - 2 MINUTES!

## ⚡ FASTEST METHOD: Netlify (Recommended)

### Step-by-Step (2 minutes):

1. **Go to Netlify**
   - Open: https://app.netlify.com/drop
   - No signup needed for first deploy!

2. **Drag & Drop**
   - Open your `portfolio` folder in Finder/Explorer
   - Drag the ENTIRE folder to the Netlify page
   - Wait 30 seconds

3. **Done!**
   - Your site is LIVE!
   - Copy the URL (e.g., `https://random-name.netlify.app`)
   - Add this URL to your resume

**That's it! Your portfolio is online! 🎉**
